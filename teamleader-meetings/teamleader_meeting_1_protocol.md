## Robot Inspection

- You will have been assigned to one arena.
- The map with the points of interest will be published.
- Entry door:
  - Set up the robot in front of it.
  - The referee will open the door.
  - The robot has to notice the open door and move through it.
  - The referee will step in front of the robot to check safety.
- Afterwards, the robot moves to the inspection points.
- You'll tell the referee about any external devices used during the test: wireless keyboards, computer, mouse, mobile phone, tray extensions for the restaurant challenge, etc.
- We'll test whether the robot is loud enough, etc.
- Once it's done, you signal the robot (however you want) to move to the exit, and we'll then push the stop button.
- Only after passing this inspection are you allowed to test.
- You'll be allowed to redo this inspection at any time during the competitions.
- During the robot inspection, you need to show us your alternative start button (not the one for opening the door) so we can write it down — it can be pressing the gripper, clicking a button, etc.
